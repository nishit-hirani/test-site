About Us

Who Are We?

Pawssible is an Ayodhya based registered NGO spreading happiness since August 2020. It started when a small group of people decided to do their bit to make the lives of stray animals better instead of turning a blind eye to their helpless state. It started as a feeding drive but now we are doing everything we can, from rescuing animals to finding them permanent home. We are a community of PAW- lovers working for our four legged friends, aiming to give them the good life they deserve as we strongly believe all animals deserve compassion and warmth. Like us, they too are entitled to the right to life, liberty and pursuit of happiness.
